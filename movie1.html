The Fellowship of the Ring

  • A guy named Sauron makes a ring and wants to take over the planet because of course he does, but he's defeated
  • Hobbit Bilbo Baggins finds the ring and holds on to it, eventually giving it to his nephew Frodo
  • The wizard Gandalf sends Frodo on a quest to destroy the ring and sends him off with some friends
  • The hobbits meet some cool folks like Aragorn and Arwen who save the hobbits from Sauron's minions
  • The hobbits make the Fellowship of the Ring (hey, title drop!) to destroy the ring
  • They are joined by Gandalf, Aragorn, Boromir, Gimli the dwarf and Legolas the elf
  • 500 hours of walking scenes commence, but the landscapes are gorgeous and the soundtrack is amazing so you don't care
  • Now they have to go through a cave and Gandalf has to sacrifice himself to stop a fire demon, many tears are shed
  • Afterwards Boromir gets corrupted by the ring, but he redeems himself and also sacrifices himself to save Frodo
  • But now Frodo and Sam are on their own, and the two other hobbits Merry and Pippin are captured!
  • So Aragorn, Legolas and Gimli decide to go save them and we end on a cliffhanger!!!
  • Great start to the trilogy, we meet a fantastic cast of characters and we get our heartstrings pulled
